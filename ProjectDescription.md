### Weather system
25% chance of weather event. Cloudy is normal
Thunderstorm = Witch trades are better (25%)
Hailstorm = Random villager death (3%)
Snowstorm = Wizard trades are better (25%)
Green rain = much easier to kill junimos (10%)
Duststorm = travel is restricted (12%)
Sun = easier to kill monsters (25%)
Clouds = normal
